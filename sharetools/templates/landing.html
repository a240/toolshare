Tool Share

A free, community powered, tool sharing app that embraces the community.

What is Tool Share

Tool Share is the future of sharing. Combining ease of use and simplicity, it brings communities together.

With Tool Share, you can find tools in your area. Need a 30 foot ladder for that tree house? Tool Share has you covered. Find yourself without in need of a few rakes for those falling leaves? Just use the Tool Share search to find rakes in your area. Need to tune a piano? With Tool Share, even finding piano tuners is a breeze.
